setDates

fun setDates(startDate: LocalDate, endDate: LocalDate?): Unit

Sets the dates to be displayed on the date pickers, and updates the selectable date ranges of each of these accordingly (i.e. their "minimum" and "maximum" dates)

The endDate may be null to represent an ongoing period of time (i.e. not ended as of present day).
